# Formation Screen Guide

## Overview

The Formation screen appears before every battle. It lets you place your units on the blue starting tiles and confirm to begin combat.

## Entering Formation

- After accepting a story battle or triggering a random encounter, the Formation screen loads automatically.
- Screen detection: `BattleFormation` (battleMode=1, slot0=0xFFFFFFFF)

## Placing Units

- Blue tiles on the battlefield are valid starting positions
- Use arrow keys to move the cursor between blue tiles
- Press Enter to place/swap a unit on the highlighted tile
- Each party member must be placed before battle can start

## Starting Battle

1. Place all units on blue tiles
2. Press Space to ready up
3. Press Enter to confirm and begin battle

## Key Bindings

| Action | Key |
|--------|-----|
| Move cursor | Arrow keys |
| Place/swap unit | Enter |
| Ready up | Space |
| Start battle | Enter (after Space) |
| Rotate camera | Q |

## Notes

- The number of blue tiles varies by map (usually 3-5)
- Unit placement affects initial positioning, which impacts first-turn range
- Units placed on high ground get height advantage
