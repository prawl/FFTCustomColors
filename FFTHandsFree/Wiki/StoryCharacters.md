# FFT Story Characters & Special Units Reference

## Ramza's Job Progression

Ramza's Squire is unique -- better stats, more equipment, and exclusive abilities compared to generic Squires. In Chapter 4, the job is renamed **Gallant Knight**.

| Chapter | Job Name | Equipment Gained | New Ability |
|---------|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| Ch1 | Squire | Robes (higher MA than generic) | Tailwind (SPD+1 to ally), Chant (HP heal) |
| Ch2 | Squire | +Shields, Heavy Armor | Steel (Brave+5 to ally) |
| Ch3 | Squire | Same as Ch2 | (no new abilities this chapter) |
| Ch4 | Gallant Knight | +Knight Swords | Shout (self: SPD+1, PA+1, MA+1, Brave+10) |

**Ultima**: Learned by getting hit by Ultima (from Assassins Celia/Lettie or Ultima Demons) while in the Squire/Gallant Knight job and surviving. Only learnable in Ch4. Best opportunity is the Limberry Castle Gate battle. 10 MP, no element, AoE damage.

**Shout** is Ramza's best ability -- self-buff only, but stacks. Use repeatedly to become unstoppable.

### Recruitment Chain for Optional Characters (Ch4)

All require **Mustadio in party**:

1. Go to Goug (cutscene) -> Go to Gollund (read rumor) -> Go to Lesalia (meet Beowulf) -> Complete Gollund Mining Town battles (4 fights) -> **Beowulf + Reis join** (+ Aquarius Stone)
2. Return to Goug with Aquarius Stone -> **Construct 8 joins**
3. Complete Nelveska Temple (bring Beowulf + Reis) -> Reis becomes human
4. Buy flower from Flower Girl at Sal Ghidos (must accept!) -> Return to Goug -> Cloud summoned -> Go to Sal Ghidos -> Win battle -> **Cloud joins**

**Balthier** (WotL only): After Bervenia battle, read "Rash of Thefts" at Gollund tavern, then "A Call for Guards" at Dorter tavern, then win the battle.

## Why Orlandeau is Broken

Orlandeau ("Thunder God Cid") joins with the **Sword Saint** job, which combines the abilities of three special knight jobs:

- **Holy Sword** (Agrias's skills): Hallowed Bolt, Judgment Blade, etc.
- **Unyielding Blade** (Meliadoul's skills): Crush Armor, Crush Weapon, etc.
- **Fell Sword** (Gaffgarion's skills): Shadowblade (drain HP), Duskblade (drain MP), etc.

All are instant (no charge time), cost no MP, and have AoE range. He joins equipped with **Excalibur** (Auto-Haste, Holy element boost). Combined with naturally high Speed and PA, he one-shots most enemies and trivializes every remaining story battle. He is widely considered the most overpowered character in SRPG history.

## Permadeath Rules

- **Generic units and permanent story characters** have a 3-turn countdown after KO. If not revived, they crystallize and are **permanently lost**.
- **Guest characters** (AI-controlled, flagged as Guest) are immune to permadeath. They auto-revive or the battle just continues.
- **Critical warning**: Story characters like Agrias, Mustadio, Rapha, Marach, Meliadoul, and Orlandeau CAN permanently die once they join your roster as regular members. They are NOT protected. Always revive them within 3 turns.
- Ramza dying = instant Game Over (no countdown).
- Construct 8 has no Faith so White Magic revive/raise will fail -- use items (Phoenix Down) or Chemist abilities.
