## Two-Handed vs Dual Wield vs Shield

| Setup | Support Ability | Effect | Best For |
|-------|----------------|--------|----------|
| Shield + Weapon | -- (default) | Physical + magic evasion | Tanks, front-liners |
| Two Hands (Doublehand) | Samurai (900 JP) | Double weapon ATK on basic attacks | High-ATK weapons (Knight Swords) |
| Dual Wield | Ninja (1000 JP) | Two weapons, attack twice | Ninjas, fast attackers |

**Decision guide:** Dual Wield gives two attacks per turn (strongest DPS). Doublehand is best with high-ATK weapons like Chaos Blade (100 effective ATK). Shield is best for survival, especially Aegis Shield vs magic-heavy enemies.

## Elemental Interactions
- **Elemental weapons** add their element to normal attacks
- If target **absorbs** that element, your attack heals them
- If target is **weak** to it, damage is doubled
- **Ice Shield** absorbs Ice, halves Fire, weak to Lightning
- **Flame Shield** absorbs Fire, halves Ice, weak to Water
- **Black Robe** boosts Fire/Ice/Lightning spell damage
- **White Robe** boosts Holy spell damage
- **Excalibur** is Holy element -- pair with White Robe wearer for boosted basic attacks
- Enemies with elemental gear can be exploited: hit Ice Shield users with Lightning
